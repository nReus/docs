## Installation

<Warning>This patch is for the **Complete Story Edition** only! It will not work on the original H2O release.</Warning>

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Open the archive">
    Using an archiving software, such as 7zip or WinRAR, open the archive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Extract the files">
    Select all of the files listed in the archive and drag it over the **root folder** of your _H2O CSE_ installation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run">
    If the game starts with the _Makura_ logo followed by the _Studio Frisay_ logo, then you've successfully installed our patch.
  </Step>
</Steps>
